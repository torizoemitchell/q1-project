Project Proposal A: Finder: Restaurants Open Late
Provide a brief description about your project that answers the following questions:

What problem or need does your project solve?
  Problem:
    finding a restaurant open late near you when traveling or when the mood strikes, and knowing what kind of food they have to offer.
  Solution:
    use google Maps to find your location and find diners near you
    return which ones are still Open
    find menus from GrubHub API to display
Who would be a user of your website?
  Night Owls or people who often travel to new cities.
What technologies will you be using?
  HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  API's: Google Maps, GrubHub
